🔬 Technology Landscape

Cell Tech: Mono PERC (95%, 22% eff) → TOPCon (25% emerging) → HJT (26% pilot)

Modules: Bifacial standard. 550-600W trackers. Half-cut cells reduce shading.

Systems: Single-axis trackers 85% utility (+25% yield). DC optimizers rare.

BESS Co-loc: Hornsdale 150MW/193MWh, Collie 500MW/2GWh, 40% new projects.

📉 Cost Reduction Trajectory

Module: $1.20→$0.22/W | EPC: $1.90→$0.82/W | LCOE: $85→$28/MWh (2015-23). AU lowest global utility PV.

🏠 Rooftop Phenomenon

4.2M systems. ACT 55% penetration. QLD 38%. $45/kW installed. VPPs (AGL 1.5GW, Origin) grid services. Feed-in tariff collapse → self-consumption 85%.

🌐 Global Exports & Industry
