Australian Energy Policy Framework

LRET, S3, REZ, Capacity Investment Scheme driving $100B+ transformation

🎯 Large-scale Renewable Energy Target (LRET)

33,000 GWh certificates by 2020 (achieved early). Created $20B industry. 6c/kWh STCs drove solar/wind boom. Now succeeded by Capacity Investment Scheme.

⚡ Safeguard Mechanism (S3)

Largest emitters buy ACCUs. Baseline-and-credit for facilities >100ktCO2. $75/t carbon price. Drives electrification, CCS, green steel.

Policy Impact

Investments: $120B committed

Jobs: 27,000 clean energy

ACCUs: 130M issued

📍 Renewable Energy Zones (REZ)

NSW: 6 zones 27 GW. VIC Central-West 4 GW. SA 6 GW. $20B transmission unlocked. Private wires + REZ declarations accelerate development.

💰 Capacity Investment Scheme

$20B government fund, private match. 32 GW firmed capacity by 2030. Tendered Sun Cable, CopperString. De-risks private investment.

🌍 Export Policies

Green H2 Guarantee of Origin scheme. Pilbara to Singapore cable (Sun Cable 20 GW). Critical minerals strategy. Future Made in Australia Act ($22B).

⏳ Timeline

2023: Capacity Scheme starts | 2025: Offshore Wind begins | 2027: REW corridors | 2030: 82% renewables | 2050: Net Zero
